Welcome to TreeSpotter!

The TreeSpotter project provides a searchable database of the trees in Vancouver. Look up your favourite tree to find its species, height, a map to its location, and everything else you could ever want to know. Want to find the tallest tree in Vancouver? Want to know how many maple trees are downtown? Just use TreeSpotter to help you learn more about the lovely trees in Vancouver.

The trees used in this database is provided by the Vancouver Parks Board.
